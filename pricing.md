---

## BYOK Always Wins

Bringing your own Claude API key or ElevenLabs API key bypasses all usage limits on every tier. We never throttle BYOK usage — you're billing yourself directly.

---

## Pricing Philosophy

- Self-hosted is genuinely free, with no artificial limits
- Cloud Free is useful enough to get people started, not a crippled demo
- Pro limits are set to cover real costs with reasonable margin (not punish power users)
- BYOK is the escape valve for anyone who hits limits and doesn't want to upgrade

---

## Open Questions

1. Should Cloud Pro users be able to bring their own Claude key to get unlimited AI without upgrading to Team?
   - *Recommendation: Yes. Builds trust, reduces churn from power users.*
2. Should self-hosted users have an optional "Folio-managed AI" add-on so they don't need to manage their own Anthropic account?
   - *Recommendation: No in V1. Too much operational complexity. Revisit at scale.*
